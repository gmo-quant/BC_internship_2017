CCN: CET1610C Title: Cisco Networking Ii Credit Hrs: 3.00
Description : 
This course describes the architecture, components, and operations of routers and switches in a small network. Students learn how to configure a router and a switch for basic for basic functionality. By the end of this course, students will be able to configure and troubleshoot routers and switches and resolve common issues with RIPv1, RIPng,single-area and multi-area OSPF, virtual LANs, and inter-VLAN routing in both IPv4 and IPv6 networks.
 
Prerequisite(s) : CET1600C
